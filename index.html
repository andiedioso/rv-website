Members

Red Velvet (Hangul: 레드벨벳) is a quintet South Korean girl group formed and managed by SM Entertainment.
Known for their powerful and harmonious vocals, the group also employs a unique dual sonic concept that reflects their own group name: their predominantly-pop "red" side experiments occasionally with electronic, funk and hip hop, while their "velvet" side focuses on '90s-influenced R&B with elements of ballad and jazz.

Irene

IRENE is the eldest and the leader of the group. She may look cold on the outside, but she has a lot of motherly love to her members.

Fast facts:
  • Birth Name: Bae Ju Hyun (배주현)
  • Birthday: March 29, 1991
  • Position: Leader, Main Rapper, Lead Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Visual, Center
  • Her designated color is pink.
The Only - IRENE feat. Raiden

Seulgi

SEULGI is the all-around ace of the group. Her sharp dance style earned her the title of Main Dancer. She also loves to draw and take photos.

Fast facts:
  • Birth Name: Kang Seul Gi (강슬기)
  • Birthday: February 10, 1994
  • Position: Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist
  • Her designated color is yellow/tangerine.
UNCOVER. sung by Seulgi

Wendy

WENDY is known as this generation's best k-pop vocalist. Music legends in Korea always compliment her for her whole vibrato and her impeccable control with her voice. She is the softest and kindest out of the five and and everyone who has had the pleasure of getting to know her also call her an angel.

Fast facts:
  • Birth Name: Son Seung Wan (손승완)
  • Birthday: February 10, 1994
  • Position: Main Vocalist
  • Her designated color is blue.
Love over a thousand years - WENDY

Joy

JOY is the second to the youngest, and like her name suggests, she always brings brightness to the room with her presence. She soothes her listeners hearts with her honey-like voice too. She absolutely loves animals. She's also starred in K-dramas and sang OSTs for them.

Fast facts:
  • Birth Name: Park Soo Young (박수영)
  • Birthday: September 3, 1996
  • Position: Lead Rapper, Sub Vocalist
  • Her designated color is green.
Introduce me a good person (Hospital Playlist OST)

Yeri

YERI is the "maknae", or the youngest, of the group. She's made a name for herself with her numerous solo songs (some of which were co-written) and now, apart from also being a brand ambassador for many products, she hosts an online variety show guesting other famous k-pop idols called 'Yeri Hanbang'. She is also a huge fan of Ariane Grande.

Fast facts:
  • Birth Name: Kim Ye Rim (김예림)
  • Birthday: March 5, 1999
  • Position: Sub Vocalist, Sub Rapper, Maknae
  • Her designated color is green.
Dear Diary - YERI

Haetnim

Haetnim, Joy's dog, is also known as Red Velvet's fluffy 6th member.
